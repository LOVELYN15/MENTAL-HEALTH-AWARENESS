Why Us?

Professional therapy services
our psychiatrists are highly trained to diagnose
treat, and counsel to prevent mental, behavioral, and
emotional disorders

Mental health is a very crucial health issue
that needs to be addressed precisely and with confidentiality
We understand this very well, our online services are
confidential and are not shared with anybody or any organization

Everyone deserves peace of mind
regardless of their financial status
we offer free online counselling for all. Call, chat
or video call one of our psychiatrists anytime anyday

We offer one on one PTSD symptoms management,
treatment and monitoring in our facilities in
Nairobi and Nakuru

We offer annual retreats to our clients to help
cool of from the pressures. Picnics offer very healthy
interactive sessions that enable sharing of experiences as well
as an opportunity for our psychiatrists to monitor our client's progress
over time
