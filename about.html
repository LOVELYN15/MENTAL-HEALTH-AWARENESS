Our Background

Mental Health Awareness organization provides one-stop access to all citizens mental health and mental health problems information.
Mental Health Awareness Organization aims to educate and guide:

  • Health and emergency preparedness professionals
  • Policy makers
  • Government and business leaders
  • Local communities
  • School systems
  • students and scholars

Our Mission

To end powerlessness in all citizens caused by pressure, poverty, mental illness, peer influence & social inequality
And empower them, regardless of background, circumstance, or history to fulfill their God given potential

Our Vision

We believe in living for more, living a life worth living
Building hope in the hopeless
Finding worth in the worthless
Choosing to live destiny conscious
