Our Background

Mental Health Awareness organization provides one-stop access
to all citizens mental health and mental health problems information.
Mental Health Awareness Organization aims to educate and guide:

  1. Health and emergency preparedness professionals
  2. Policy makers
  3. Government and business leaders
  4. Local communities
  5. School systems
  6. students and scholars

Content for this website is provided by: Read More

Our Mission

To end powerlessness in all citizens caused by pressure, poverty, mental illness, peer influence & social inequality
And empower them, regardless of background, circumstance, or history to fulfill their God given potential

Our Vision

  1. We believe in living for more, living a life worth living
  2. Building hope in the hopeless
  3. Finding worth in the worthless
  4. Choosing to live destiny conscious
